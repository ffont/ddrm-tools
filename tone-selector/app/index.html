About

This CS80-like tone slector allows you to use the presetting system of CS80 in DDRM. Simply click on the buttons of the upper row and lower row to configure slider positions for channel I and II respectively. Have fun!

DDRM CS80 Tone Selector communicates with DDRM via MIDI. Make sure your DDRM is connected to the computer and can receive MIDI messages at this port:
